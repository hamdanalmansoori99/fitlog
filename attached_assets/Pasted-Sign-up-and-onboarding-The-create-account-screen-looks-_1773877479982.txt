Biggest weaknesses now

The app is better, but I still can’t reduce it to one unforgettable sentence.

It should be something like:
“The workout tracker that tells you exactly what to do next.”

Right now it risks being:
“a nice-looking all-in-one fitness app.”

That’s not enough.

2. Meals and workouts still feel slightly split

They need stronger product unification.

3. AI still needs to prove itself

This should be your killer edge, and I’m not fully convinced yet.

4. Too much value is distributed across tabs

The home screen should feel more decisive and central.
